Converting your points to a discount coupon

If your total rental points are 50,000 P or less, you may request the conversion to discount coupons for rental. (General Member 50% discount coupon / Gold Member 55% discount coupon / Double Gold Member 60% discount coupon)

ex) 25,000 point for 1 discount tickets Deduct → 10,000 point for a discount tickets and remaining 15,000 point can be used for another discount tickets subsequently
