Mainland Reservation Information

  • Choosing Rental Period
    • Reservation is available up to 60 days from pick date.(but, Incheon airport branch is available up to 90 days)
    • Minimum 24 hours of rental period is required to apply discount.
  • Choosing a Location
    • You can choose either General (choose a specific model), or Mystery Deal (choose car group only)
    • Mystery Deal is available for members only.
  • Choosing a Car
    • You can choose either General (choose a specific model), or Mystery Deal (choose car group only)
    • Mystery Deal is available for members only.
  • Internet Booking
    • Additional 5% discount will be applied to the customers booking through Lotte Rent-a-Car homepage.
  • Members Booking
    • Members of Lotte Rent-a-Car can get 30% to 55% discount with other members benefit..
    • 01. Login with your ID and PW
    • 02. Select Pickup/Return date and time, Location, and vehicle.
    • 03. Compare and choose your prefered deal.
    • 04. Select additional options (GPS, CDW, and etc), and check the price.
    • 05. Check rental requirement, payment information, and other information then proceed reservation.
    • 06. Check your reservation number and rental information.
    • 07. Your booking is complete!
  • Non-members Booking
    • 01. Select Pickup/Return date and time, Location, and vehicle.
    • 02. Compare and choose your prefered deal.
    • 03. Select additional options (GPS, CDW, and etc), and check the price.
    • 04. Check rental requirement, payment information, and other information then proceed reservation.
    • 05. Check your reservation number and rental information.
    • 06. Your booking is complete!
  • Assistance for booking and payment.
    • Please call our customer center (1588-1230), and English speaking assistant will serve you.
      • Customer Center hours: 08:30~20:00 (365 days)
  • Booking at the location
    • You can visit our locations to book a car. Our assistant will help you with booking and choose a car that you want to drive.
    • Operations hours are vary for each location. Please check hours of your prefered location beforehand.

Delivery Service (DS)

Information for Delivery Service (DS)

Target Lotte rent-a-car member(Non-members can use Delivery Service after sign up for membership.)
Amount used Price: 15,000KRW / addition to rental fare
Payment method Creditcard with renter's name is required(corporate creditcard is available)
Serviceability Time and locations
  • morning 09:30~18:00 (Minimum 48 hours of lead time is required.)
  • With less than 48 hours of rental period, please contact to your selected location for availability.
    • Gangnam (02-3443-8000)
    • Nonhyeon (02-511-1001)
    • Jamsil (02-2203-8000)
    • Express Bus Terminal (02-3444-8000)
    • Guro (02-3664-8000)
    • Gangbuk (02-922-8000)
    • Seoul Station (02-715-0010)
    • Yeouido (02-785-8000)
  • Delivery service is not available when the service is lost.Customer contacts Please fill in exactly
  • DS (Delivery Service) is for members only. (You can sign up for membership to use our DS)
  • If you choose i-PIN for verification, we might ask you for your residence id# to complete the Rental Agreement.
  • Reservation must be made by the renter. We might not able to provide DS if the actual renter did not make the reservaiton.
  • Service hours are different according to your location. (available area for DS: Entire Seoul)
  • DS rate is 15,000KRW (per rent), and additional fee will be charged if mileage exceeds 20km.
  • DS is payable with creditcard (or point) only with renter's name on it.
    (Payment can not be made for cash (cash / free pass) accompanied by credit information when rental vehicles are rented.)
  • Due to preparation of a vehicle and the driver, reservation must be made at least 48 hour prior to the pick up. The service may be limited according to the request of schedule change.
  • The vehicle shall arrive on your locaiton within 30 minutes from requested time depends on traffic.
  • Rental period starts from the moment that you sign on the rental agreement.
  • DS rate will be charged to the if a customer cancels DS due to change of mind.
  • Booking time and location changes can be made only three hours prior to the appointment, and you must inquire directly to the relevant point. 
  • DS is a proactive call to location & Customer Center when Early / returning delay / period extension. 
    • If you have not contacted location & Customer Center, the DS service may be limited.
