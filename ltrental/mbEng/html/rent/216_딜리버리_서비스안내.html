Information for Delivery Service (DS)

Target Lotte rent-a-car member
(Non-members can use Delivery Service after sign up for membership.)
Amount used Price: 15,000KRW / addition to rental fare
Payment method Creditcard with renter's name is required
(corporate creditcard is available)
Serviceability Time and locations

  • DS (Delivery Service) is for members only. (You can sign up for membership to use our DS)
  • If you choose i-PIN for verification, we might ask you for your residence id# to complete the Rental Agreement.
  • Reservation must be made by the renter. We might not able to provide DS if the actual renter did not make the reservaiton.
  • Service hours are different according to your location. (available area for DS: Entire Seoul)
  • DS rate is 15,000KRW (per rent), and additional fee will be charged if mileage exceeds 20km.
  • DS is payable with creditcard (or point) only with renter's name on it.(Payment can not be made for cash (cash / free pass) accompanied by credit information when rental vehicles are rented.)
  • Due to preparation of a vehicle and the driver, reservation must be made at least 48 hour prior to the pick up. The service may be limited according to the request of schedule change.
  • The vehicle shall arrive on your locaiton within 30 minutes from requested time depends on traffic.
  • Rental period starts from the moment that you sign on the rental agreement.
  • DS rate will be charged to the if a customer cancels DS due to change of mind.
  • Booking time and location changes can be made only three hours prior to the appointment, and you must inquire directly to the relevant point.
  • DS is a proactive call to location & Customer Center when Early / returning delay / period extension.
    • If you have not contacted location & Customer Center, the DS service may be limited.
