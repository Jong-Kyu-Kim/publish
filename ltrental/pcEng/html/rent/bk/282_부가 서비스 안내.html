Additional Rate

Rental Service

Experience a range of additional options, free or paid, offered from LOTTE rent-a-car!

  • Navigation Rental Service (Free)
    • All LOTTE rent-a-car locations have navigation. Pre-reservation for navigation rental is available.
  • Infant Car Seat Rental Service (paid)
    • Available at LOTTE rent-a-car locations nationwide
    • Available through prior reservation
    • Car seat installation may be difficult depending on the type of rented vehicle
  • One-Way Rental(paid)
    • One-way rental service is available if you would like to return the rented car to different location, not the original location.
    • Available when the vehicle is rented for more than 24 hours/ additional charges may apply depending on the mileage.
