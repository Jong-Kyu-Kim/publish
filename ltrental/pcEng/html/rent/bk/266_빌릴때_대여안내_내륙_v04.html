Rental Requirements

  • General Requirements (up to 9 passengers)
    • Minimum 21 years of age
    • Valid international driving permit, Class 2 or higher
    • The international driving permit must have been issued at least one (1) year prior to the rental date
  • Van Rental Requirements (11 passengers, 12 passengers)
    • Minimum 21 years of age
    • Valid international driving permit, Class 1 or higher
    • The international driving permit must have been issued at least one (1) year prior to the rental date
  • 15 passengers (CAUNTY Limousine)
    • Must rent with a driver
    • Inquiry : 02-547-8000
  • Under the Passenger Transport Act Regulations Article 67, LOTTE rent-a-car provides only vehicles for 15 passengers or less.
  • The driver's license must be valid for the entire rental period.
  • In case of the international driving permit class 1, the license expires a year after the aptitude test; and rental is not available with the expired international driving permit.

Rental Procedures

  • 01. Visit a local branch
    • On the day of car pick-up, visit the branch and give our agent in charge your reservation number or name under which the reservation is made
    • If you are foreigner, Passport and international driving permit are required for identification.
  • 02. Confirm customer information
    • Check the reservation information and present your international driving permit so that we can confirm the customer information for preparing the Car rental Agreement.
  • 03. Make a payment
    • After confirmation is completed on rental period, type of car, and options, you are asked to pay the stated rental fee. * Your own credit card is the first priority as means of payment.
    • You will get back your ID card, international driving permit, credit card, etc, which you presented to the agency in charge
  • 04. Sign contract
    • Check the car in the parking garage ready to go, and execute and sign the car rental agreement guided by our agent.
    • Once you receive a copy of the Agreement and the key, you are ready to use the car.

Return Procedures

  • 01. Visit a local branch
    • Park the car in the parking garage of the branch where the car is to be returned, stop the engine, take out your belongings and enter the office carrying the car key with you.
  • 02. Confirm the final documents and return the key
    • Hand over the key and the copy of the car rental agreement to the agent in charge.
  • 03. Vehicle Inspection
    • Accompany the agent in charge to the parking place, who will check the interior and exterior, operation and the fuel gauge of the car.
  • 04. Assess for additional fees
    • In case of late returning, any additional costs such as loss or damage to the car, and traffic fines, you are asked to pay additional fee as relevant.
    • Once the process for returning the rental car is completed, you may leave as guided by the agent in charge.

Insurance & Other Coverages in the Event of Auto Accident

LOTTE rent-a-car endeavors to protect all customers in case of auto accidents through the insurance and coverages below.

Auto Accident Car Damage Waiver (CDW)

All losses or damages to the rented vehicle during the rental period, for any reason attributable to the customer, is the customer’s responsibility.
However, Car Damage Waiver (CDW) is an optional service which offers protection from financial liability for losses or damages to the rented vehicle through your fault.

Deductibles in Auto Accident Car Damage Waiver

Paying 50,000- 300,000 won per accident can protect you from the liability for losses or damages to the car, regardless of the amount. However, the fee may vary by Car Damage Waiver Deductible policy

Types of Deductibles Policy

  • Domestic (Korean) Vehicle

  • Imported Vehicle

  • Depending on your deductible policy, you will be responsible for the cost of the vehicle damage if the repair cost is less than the deductible. If the cost of the damage exceeds the deductible, you are only responsible for your policy deductible.

Jeju Deductible Policy

  • Domestic (Korean) Vehicle

  • Imported Vehicle

  • Regardless of the cost of loss or damage, Car Damage Waiver (CDW) protects you and relieves of the liability for loss or damage to the car.

Liability Exemption Policy at 7 Mainland Airport Locations

Available Locations Gimpo International Airport, Incheon International Air port, Gimhae International Airport, Sacheon Airport, Pohang Airport, Gwangju Airport, Yeosu Airport locations
Customer Liability Liability Exemption, 50,000won, 300,000won are available

Vehicle Repair Costs

All losses or damages to the rented vehicle, for any reason attributable to the customer, during the rental period is the customer’s responsibility. (However, Car Damage Waiver (CDW) is an optional service which offers protection from  financial liability for losses or damages to the rented vehicle through your fault. )

Compensation for an Inactive Vehicle

Whether a customer signs up for CDW or not, the customer is responsible for losses caused by disruption in car rental service for the duration of repair. In such case, the customer will be charged for compensation for an inactive vehicle,  which is 50% of normal rental fee of the damaged vehicle.
