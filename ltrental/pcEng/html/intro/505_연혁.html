History

LOTTE Rental operates as a general rental company With 30 years of corporate history(established in May 1986).

2010 No. 1 provider of car rental Kumho Rent-A-Car has merged , incorporated in June 2015 and Lotte Group has grown into Korea's leading rental companies

2010~2015

Aug 2015 Company names and brand names changed to LOTTE rental and LOTTE rent a car
May 2014 Kumho Launched Kumho rent a car 'Auto House'
Mar 2014 Launched kumho rental 'Auto Auction'
Oct 2013 Acquire shares in Green car and extention car sharing serviece
Mar 2013 Foundation Autocare from kumho rental
Dec 2011 LOTTE Autolease established
June 2010 M&A with Rent-a-Car Division of Kumho Rent-a-Car

2000~2009

Oct 2005 Kumho Rental established as an independent entity
Apr 2004 Merged with kt Networks
Feb 2004 Began car rental business
Aug 2003 Changed to kt Rental Co., Ltd.
Jan 2001 Designated as the managing company of the distribution of mid- and large-scale computers under the Informatization Promotion Fund

1986~1999

Dec 1999 Centel Inc. established
Dec 1987 Rental business of IT equipment launched
May 1986 Korea Informatics Telesis Inc. established
