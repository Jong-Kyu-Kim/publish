  • Rental Eligibility
    • Small/medium/van of 7 or 9 passenger capacity – Over 21 years of age, with 1 year of longer driving experience
    • Van of 10 passenger capacity – Over 21 years of age, with 1 years of longer driving experience
    • Rental is not allowed if no effective period for aptitude test is remaining within the rental period.
    • If you seek registration of a second driver, he or she must accompany you carrying his/her international driving permit when you pick up the car.
    • If you are foreigner, Passport and international driving permit are required for identification.
    • If the car driver does not meet the eligibility as above, car rental may be rejected on the site. So, please check the eligibility beforehand.
  • International driving permit
    • International driving permit (must be presented to pick up the car) – those who have international driving permit effective under the Road Traffic Act
    • For a foreigner, only those who have international driving permit(Grade B – vehicles with 9 or less passenger capacity)
    • Please check availability for your international driving permit or your country to rent a car in Korea.
    • If you are not from any countries in the Geneva Conventions, it is not allowed to rent a car.
  • Insurance
    • All of our cars are insured with comprehensive coverage (person, property, own damage), with CDW offered as option upon picking up the car at the relevant branch.
  • Payment Terms
    • Form of payment, the main driver has to pay by own credit card (No debit card, cash and dollar) when pick it up at related location.
    • If not, we accept a travelling companion’s credit card that the one should be with the main driver during the rental period.
    • Payment for rental charge should be by credit card of the renter’s own in principle.
    • If you fail to show up at the branch by the reserved time without any prior notice, your reservation will be cancelled and your points reduced by 1,000
