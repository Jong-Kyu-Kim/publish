Additional Fee Information

  • Compensation for an Inactive Vehicle
    • If the rented vehicle needs to be repaired from the accident (the lessee at fault), the customer is responsible for the loss of revenue for the rental car during the repair period. In such case, the customer will be charged for compensation for an inactive vehicle, which is 50% of normal rental fee.
  • Vehicle Repair Cost
    • In the case of damages to, or loss of, the vehicle during the rental period (caused through the fault of the lessee or unknown) the customer is liable for the rented vehicle repair cost. The repair must take place at a designated place through agreement with LOTTE rent-a-car., except under special circumstances.
  • Traffic Violation Penalty
    • The customer will be liable for any penalty charge incurred from traffic violations (Parking, bus lane, seat belt violation, etc.) during the rental period. In the event of traffic violations, you may not be able to partially or entirely benefit from insurance and other coverage.
  • Refueling Service Charge
    • All vehicles are rented with a Full Tank (the vehicle has full amount of fuel), and must be returned with a Full Tank. If the vehicle does not have required amount of fuel, please refuel at the nearest gas station, or pay the refueling service charge
    • refueling service charge will be based on the rate set on the fuel cost chart, placed on the front desk at the rental location
