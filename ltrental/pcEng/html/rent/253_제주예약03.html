    • If you are foreigner, Passport and international driving permit are required for identification.
    • If the car driver does not meet the eligibility as above, car rental may be rejected on the site. So, please check the eligibility beforehand.
  • International driving permit
    • International driving permit (must be presented to pick up the car)
